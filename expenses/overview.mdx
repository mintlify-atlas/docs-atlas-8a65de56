# Expense management

Frappe HR gives you a complete expense management workflow. Employees submit claims and advance requests, approvers review and sanction them, and finance pays out reimbursements — all linked to the accounting ledger.

## Expense lifecycle

The typical expense lifecycle in Frappe HR moves through these stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan">
    The employee creates a **Travel Request** before a business trip, listing the itinerary and estimated costs. Management can approve or reject the request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request an advance">
    If the employee needs cash upfront, they create an **Employee Advance**. Once approved and paid, the advance status becomes **Paid**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Incur expenses">
    The employee spends money and collects receipts during the trip or event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit an expense claim">
    On return, the employee creates an **Expense Claim**, lists each expense line, attaches receipts, and links any outstanding **Employee Advance** to offset the amount owed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approval">
    The designated **Expense Approver** reviews the claim. They can sanction the full claimed amount or a lower amount per line, then set **Approval Status** to **Approved** or **Rejected**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment">
    After submission, finance pays the outstanding balance via a **Payment Entry** or **Journal Entry**. The claim status moves to **Paid** once the full amount is reimbursed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key documents

| Document | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Expense Claim Type** | Defines expense categories (e.g. Travel, Meals) and maps each to a ledger account per company |
| **Employee Advance** | Records a cash advance paid to an employee before expenses are incurred |
| **Expense Claim** | The primary reimbursement request — lists individual expense lines and links to advances |
| **Travel Request** | Pre-trip approval document with itinerary and cost estimates |

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Employee** | Create and submit their own expense claims and advance requests |
| **Expense Approver** | Read, write, submit, cancel, and amend expense claims and advances |
| **HR User** | Full access to expense claims for oversight |
| **HR Manager** | Full access including deletion and approval-level fields |

<Note>
You can configure a **prevent self-approval** setting in **HR Settings** to stop employees from approving their own expense claims.
</Note>
