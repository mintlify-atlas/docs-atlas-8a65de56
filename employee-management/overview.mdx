## How the modules fit together

All employee management documents share a common pattern:

1. You create and submit a document (Onboarding, Transfer, Promotion, or Separation).
2. Frappe HR updates the **Employee** record automatically based on the submitted document.
3. A history entry is recorded in the employee's internal work history so you can audit every change.

<Note>
Most documents in this section are **submittable** — they must be submitted before they take effect on the employee record. Cancelling a submitted document reverses the changes.
</Note>
