## The payroll cycle

Each payroll run follows the same end-to-end sequence:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up salary components">
    Create **Salary Components** for each earning (Basic, HRA, Allowances) and deduction (Provident Fund, Income Tax). Components can carry a fixed amount or a Python formula evaluated at runtime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define salary structures">
    Assemble components into a **Salary Structure**. Each structure is scoped to a company and currency and specifies the payroll frequency (Monthly, Weekly, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign structures to employees">
    Create a **Salary Structure Assignment** linking an employee to a structure with an effective **From Date** and a **Base** salary amount. The assignment also specifies the **Income Tax Slab** for the employee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a payroll entry">
    Open a **Payroll Entry** for the period, filter eligible employees, then use **Get Employees** to populate the employee list. Submit the entry to trigger bulk salary slip creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and submit salary slips">
    Review any generated **Salary Slips** before bulk-submitting them. During submission, an accrual journal entry is automatically posted to the configured **Payroll Payable Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the bank payment entry">
    After slip submission, use **Make Bank Entry** on the payroll entry to create a bank journal entry that clears the payroll payable account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| **Salary Component** | An individual earning or deduction item with an optional formula |
| **Salary Structure** | A template that groups components for a given company and frequency |
| **Salary Structure Assignment** | Links an employee to a structure with a base salary and effective date |
| **Payroll Period** | A date range (usually a fiscal year) used for income tax computation |
| **Income Tax Slab** | Tax rate brackets applied against an employee's annual taxable income |
| **Additional Salary** | A one-time or recurring adjustment outside the regular structure |
| **Gratuity** | End-of-service benefit calculated from years of service |

<Note>
Frappe HR requires ERPNext for accounting. Ensure your **Payroll Payable Account** and salary component accounts are configured before your first payroll run.
</Note>
