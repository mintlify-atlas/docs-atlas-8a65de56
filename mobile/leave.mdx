The **Leaves** tab gives you a full view of your leave balance, recent applications, and upcoming holidays. Managers can also review and act on their team's leave requests from the same interface.

## Viewing your leave balance

Tap **Leaves** in the bottom tab bar to open the Leaves & Holidays dashboard.

At the top of the screen you see a leave balance panel. Each allocated leave type appears as a semicircle chart showing **remaining / total** days. For example, if you have 8 of 12 Casual Leave days remaining, the chart shows `8/12`.

Tap **View Leave History** next to the Leave Balance heading to open the full leave list.

## Applying for leave

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the leave form">
    On the **Leaves & Holidays** dashboard, tap **Request a Leave**. You can also tap **Request Leave** from the quick links on the **Home** screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select leave type and dates">
    Choose a **Leave Type** from the list of types allocated to you. Set the **From Date** and **To Date**. The app automatically sets **To Date** equal to **From Date** when you first choose the start date.

    If you need a half day, enable **Half Day** and select the specific **Half Day Date** within your date range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the leave balance">
    After selecting the leave type and dates, the form shows the **Leave Balance** available for the selected period and calculates **Total Leave Days** automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a leave approver">
    If your department requires a leave approver, the **Leave Approver** field is pre-filled with the approver linked to your department. You can change it to any eligible approver from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a reason">
    Enter a reason in the **Description** field (optional unless your leave type requires it).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach a document">
    If you need to attach supporting documents (for example, a medical certificate), tap the attachment section at the bottom of the form and select a file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the application">
    Tap **Submit** to send the leave application for approval. The status changes to **Open**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The app only shows leave types that have an active allocation for you on the selected start date. If a leave type does not appear in the list, check that an allocation exists for the current period.
</Note>

## Viewing leave history

From the **Leaves & Holidays** dashboard, tap **View Leave History** or scroll down to **Recent Leaves** and tap **View List**.

The leave list has two tabs:

- **My Leaves** — your own leave applications
- **Team Leaves** — leave applications from employees you manage

You can filter the list by **Status** (Open, Approved, Rejected), **Leave Type**, **Employee**, **Department**, **From Date**, and **To Date**.

Tap any entry to open the full leave application form.

## Approving or rejecting leave requests

If you are a leave approver, your team members' leave requests appear under the **Team Leaves** tab.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the team leave request">
    Go to **Leaves** > **View List** > **Team Leaves**, then tap the leave application you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the details">
    The form opens in read-only mode showing the employee, leave type, dates, balance, and reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or reject">
    Tap **Approve** to approve the leave or **Reject** to decline it. The employee's leave balance updates accordingly after approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You receive a push notification when an employee submits a leave request that requires your approval. Make sure push notifications are enabled in **Profile** > **Settings**.
</Tip>

## Upcoming holidays

Below the recent leaves list, the dashboard shows the **Holidays** section with the next upcoming holidays from your assigned holiday list. Holidays are read-only and reflect the holiday calendar configured for your company or branch.
