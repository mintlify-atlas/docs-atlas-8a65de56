The **Salary** tab gives you access to your salary slips from your mobile browser. You can view the earnings and deductions breakdown for any period and download slips as a PDF.

## Salary slips dashboard

Tap **Salary** in the bottom tab bar to open the salary slips dashboard.

At the top of the screen you see your **Year to Date** gross pay figure — the total gross salary accumulated from the start of the current payroll year to the most recently submitted slip.

Below that is a **Payroll Period** selector. It defaults to the most recent payroll period for your company. Change it to browse slips from earlier periods.

The list below the selector shows all submitted salary slips for the selected payroll period, ordered by end date (most recent first). Each row displays:

- The slip's date range
- **Gross Pay**
- **Net Pay**
- The currency

<Note>
Only submitted (finalised) salary slips appear in the list. Draft slips processed during an in-progress payroll run do not appear until they are submitted by your payroll administrator.
</Note>

## Viewing a salary slip

Tap any salary slip in the list to open the detail view. The detail view is organised into tabs:

| Tab | Contents |
|-----|----------|
| **Details** | Pay period, payment days, working days, and absent days |
| **Earnings & Deductions** | Itemised earnings and deductions components with amounts |
| **Net Pay Info** | Gross pay, total deductions, net pay, and net pay in words |
| **Income Tax Breakup** | Monthly income tax, total income tax, and year-to-date tax |
| **Bank Details** | Bank name, account number, and IFSC code |

## Earnings and deductions breakdown

Switch to the **Earnings & Deductions** tab to see the itemised breakdown.

- **Earnings** lists each earning component (for example, Basic, HRA, Conveyance Allowance) with its amount for the pay period.
- **Deductions** lists each deduction component (for example, Provident Fund, Professional Tax, TDS) with its amount.

If your salary slip is in a currency different from your company's base currency, the tab shows amounts in both currencies.

<Tip>
Fields labelled with the company's base currency (for example, "Amount (INR)") are hidden when your salary currency already matches the company currency, keeping the view uncluttered.
</Tip>

## Downloading a salary slip

From any open salary slip detail view, tap **Download PDF** at the bottom of the screen. The app fetches the PDF from the server and downloads it to your device using the slip name as the filename.

<Note>
The PDF is generated server-side using Frappe's print format engine. If the download button shows an error, check that the print format is configured correctly in the desktop interface.
</Note>

## Real-time updates

The salary slips dashboard listens for a `hrms:update_salary_slips` socket event. When your payroll administrator submits or updates your salary slips, the list refreshes automatically without you needing to reload the page.
