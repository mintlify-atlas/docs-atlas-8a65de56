The **Expenses** tab lets you submit expense claims, attach receipt photos, monitor approval status, and review employee advance balances — all from your phone.

## Expense claims dashboard

Tap **Expenses** in the bottom tab bar to open the dashboard. It shows:

- **Expense Claim Summary** — a summary of your pending and approved claims
- **Claim an Expense** button — opens the new claim form
- **Recent Expenses** — your latest expense claims with their approval status
- **Employee Advance Balance** — outstanding advance amounts linked to your account

## Creating an expense claim

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the expense claim form">
    Tap **Claim an Expense** on the Expenses dashboard, or tap **Claim an Expense** from the quick links on the **Home** screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add expense line items">
    The form opens on the **Expenses** tab. Tap **Add Row** to add an expense item. For each item, enter:
    - **Expense Type** — the category of the expense (e.g., Travel, Meals)
    - **Expense Date** — the date the expense was incurred
    - **Amount** — the claimed amount
    - **Description** — a brief note about the expense (optional)

    Repeat this for each expense item in the claim. The form calculates the **Total Claimed Amount** automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply taxes (optional)">
    Switch to the **Advances** tab if you need to apply taxes and charges. Add rows in the taxes table with the applicable tax rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allocate advances (optional)">
    If you have outstanding employee advances, they appear in the **Advances** tab. Select any advance to allocate its unclaimed amount against this claim. The **Grand Total** updates to reflect the net payable after advance deduction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review totals">
    Switch to the **Totals** tab to verify the **Total Claimed Amount**, taxes, advance deductions, and **Grand Total**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an expense approver">
    The **Expense Approver** field is pre-filled with the approver linked to your department. Change it if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach receipts">
    Scroll to the attachment section at the bottom of the form and tap to attach receipt photos or scanned documents. You can attach multiple files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the claim">
    Tap **Submit** to send the expense claim for approval. The approval status changes to **Submitted**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The **Posting Date** defaults to today's date. The **Company**, **Cost Center**, and **Payable Account** fields are auto-filled from your employee profile. You can change them if your organisation allows it.
</Note>

## Attaching receipt photos

The expense claim form includes an attachment view at the bottom of the form. Tap the attachment area to:

- Choose a file from your device's gallery or file manager
- Take a new photo directly with your camera (on supported browsers)

Attached receipts are stored against the expense claim document and are visible to the approver.

<Tip>
Most mobile browsers support camera access for file inputs. If the camera option does not appear, check that your browser has camera permission.
</Tip>

## Viewing claim status

From the Expenses dashboard, tap **View List** under Recent Expenses to open the full claim history. The list has two tabs:

- **My Claims** — your own expense claims
- **Team Claims** — claims submitted by employees you approve

You can filter by **Approval Status** (Draft, Approved, Rejected), **Status** (Draft, Paid, Unpaid, Rejected, Submitted, Cancelled), **Employee**, **Department**, and **Posting Date**.

Tap any claim to open it and see the full breakdown of items, taxes, advances, and totals.

## Approving expense claims

If you are listed as the expense approver for a team member, their submitted claims appear under **Team Claims**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the team claim">
    Go to **Expenses** > **View List** > **Team Claims**, then tap the claim you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the claim">
    The form opens in read-only mode. Review the expense items, attached receipts, totals, and advance allocations across the **Expenses**, **Advances**, and **Totals** tabs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or reject">
    Tap **Approve** to approve the claim or **Reject** to decline it. The approval status updates immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading the expense claim as PDF

When viewing a submitted expense claim, tap **Download PDF** to generate and download a PDF copy of the claim to your device.
